About Us

Who We Are

Fastfood is an English word that means "fast food".
This is the common name for foods that are prepared and
served to people quickly. Any food or meal that is served
quickly can be considered Fastfood.
With more than 100 sandwich shops across the country, We have
become a popular fast food restaurant chain in the world. Our traditional
dishes are sandwiches (burgers) and salads. This group is currently
headquartered in Ngu Hanh Son District, Da Nang City.
